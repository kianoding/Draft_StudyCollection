About The Collection

Pratt School of Fashion: Study Collection

The Fashion Archives and Research Collections at Pratt Institute: Our digital repository bridges historical fashion and contemporary scholarship through a comprehensive collection of design artifacts. The archive preserves and presents significant garments, textiles, and accessories that chronicle the evolution of fashion design and material culture. By transforming our physical collections into digital resources, we provide scholars, designers, and researchers worldwide with direct access to primary source materials illuminating fashion history's rich narrative. Through this digital initiative, we aim to advance the scholarly discourse surrounding design methodology, cultural significance, and the evolving language of fashion.

Source: Pratt School of Design - Fashion Design. https://www.pratt.edu/design/fashion-design
